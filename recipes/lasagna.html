Lasagna

Finished dish

Ingredients

  • 9 precooked lasagna noodles
  • 500 g (1.1 lb) ground beef (or pork)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 200 g (7 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 200 g (7 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until golden and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through. Break up the meat with a wooden spoon while cooking.
  4. Add the grated carrot to the skillet and cook for a few minutes until tender.
  5. Pour the crushed tomatoes into the skillet and mix well. Add the dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper to taste. Cook over medium-low heat for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with half of the grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat mixture. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top. Then, add a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture and sprinkle some shredded mozzarella cheese over it.
  8. Repeat this process, alternating layers of meat, lasagna noodles, and ricotta cheese, until all the ingredients are used. Be sure to finish with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
  11. Then, remove the aluminum foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  12. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into portions and serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Meat and Cheese Lasagna

Feel free to customize this basic recipe by adding more ingredients like spinacj, mushrooms, or bell peppers if you like. Enjoy your meal.
