Pizza

Finished dish

Ingredients:

  • 11 pre-made pizza crust (or pizza dough)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • HFresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven according to the instructions on the pizza crust or dough package.
  2. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the pizza crust.
  3. Generously sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  4. Place the sliced tomatoes evenly over the cheese.
  5. Add some fresh basil leaves on top of the tomatoes.
  6. Drizzle a little extra virgin olive oil over the pizza.
  7. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven according to the instructions on the pizza crust or dough package, usually for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Once out of the oven, let the pizza cool slightly before slicing into portions.
  10. Serve and savor your delicious homemade Margherita Pizza.

Feel free to customize this classic Margherita Pizza with your favorite toppings and enjoy a taste of Italy in your own kitchen!
