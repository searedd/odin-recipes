Ramen

Finished dish

Ingredients:

  • 2 blocks of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken broth (homemade or store-bought)
  • 200 g cooked chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions (optional)
  • Hard-boiled eggs (optional)
  • Nori seaweed strips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process.
  2. Heat the chicken broth in a large pot over medium heat. Add soy sauce and sesame oil to flavor the broth.
  3. Add the sliced carrots to the broth and cook for a few minutes until they are tender but still crisp.
  4. Stir in the fresh spinach and cook until it wilts.
  5. Divide the cooked ramen noodles into individual bowls.
  6. Pour the hot broth over the noodles, making sure the vegetables are evenly distributed.
  7. Place the sliced cooked chicken on top of each bowl.
  8. If desired, add chopped green onions, halved hard-boiled eggs, and nori seaweed strips for extra flavor and texture.
  9. Serve the hot ramen and enjoy this comforting soup.

This homemade chicken and vegetable ramen is quick and easy to make, and you can customize it by adding your favorite ingredients. Enjoy your meal!
